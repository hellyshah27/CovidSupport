About CovidSupport

CovidSupport is the platform which provides helps the covid affected people. Using CovidSupport, the patients and help providers will connect with each other. Patients can post their emergency need into this portal. Also help providers/ NGOs can post that what they have for the covid affected patients to help.

There are many number of NGOs and help providers are already working for the covid affected people but people are not aware of that and people are also do not know how to contact them for the emergency need. So this platform provides the help to patients, help providers and NGOs also.

UN Sustainability Development Goals




COVIDSUPPORT MISSION

CovidSupport platform is concentrate the UN goal number 3 which is for Healthy life and Well Being. There are 17 UN sustainabilty development goals, but we are focusing on the one which is about ensuring healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages because of the current situation about the pandemic. Covid-19 is affecting more and more number of people and some of the nations are facing difficulties so this CovidSupport will provide them help in some of the way.

Ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being at all ages is essential to sustainable development. Currently, the world is facing a global health crisis unlike any other — COVID-19 is spreading human suffering, destabilizing the global economy and upending the lives of billions of people around the globe.

Health emergencies such as COVID-19 pose a global risk and have shown the critical need for preparedness. The United Nations Development Programme highlighted huge disparities in countries’ abilities to cope with and recover from the COVID-19 crisis. The pandemic provides a watershed moment for health emergency preparedness and for investment in critical 21st century public services.

WHAT COVIDSUPPORT OFFERS

NGO LIST

This section have the list of the NGOs who already working for the corona patients. By which patients or their relatives will come to know about them and also contact them for their emergency need. This will solve the problem of patients that they do not need to call randomly for help.

HELP PROVIDERS LIST

This is the list of help providers who are not the NGOs but they voluntarily want to help the corona patients and their families who are facing difficulty to having treatment for the covid.

PATIENTS LIST

This section will have the list of all the patients who are registered for the emergency need. Help providers and NGOs will able to see the list of all the patients who need an emergency help. So help providers or NGOs are also able to contact them and connect with them aspecially when in the case of rural areas.
